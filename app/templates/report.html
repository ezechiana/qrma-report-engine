How to read this dashboard

This dashboard provides a weighted clinical overview of the scan. The Health Index and body-system cards are not based on a simple count of flagged markers. They are derived from the severity, distribution, and clustering of findings across related sections, with greater weighting given to patterns that may carry broader functional significance.

Each body-system card may include more than one underlying scan section, which is why the totals shown here may differ from the marker counts shown later on individual section pages. In general, lower scores suggest a more active pattern worth reviewing in follow-up, while higher scores suggest a more stable pattern on this dashboard view.

These scores are designed to help organise clinical attention. They are not a diagnosis and should always be interpreted alongside symptoms, history, and appropriate clinical assessment.
