Questions

Common launch questions.

Who is go360 for?

go360 is designed for practitioners and wellness scan providers using QRMA-style scan exports who want a more professional way to interpret, present, share, and monetise reports.

Does go360 diagnose disease?

No. go360 is designed for wellness reporting, education, practitioner review, and client communication. It should not be used as a substitute for medical diagnosis or treatment.

Can I share reports with clients?

Yes. You can create secure share links and report bundles, with options such as expiry, password protection, and paid access workflows.

What happens after the free trial?

You can continue viewing existing reports and shares. Creating new reports, share links, and bundles requires an active Practitioner Pro subscription.

Is Spanish available?

The first production version is being completed in English. Spanish client-facing reports are planned after the English workflow is stable with pilot users.

Do I need a credit card to start?

The launch trial is designed to let practitioners evaluate the workflow before upgrading to the monthly plan.
